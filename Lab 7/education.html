My Education

Aspee Nutan Vidya Mandir High School

Mathematics have always been my favorite class during my high school. One of the reason is I love solving problems and this class used to help me regain my energy after theory classes.

Memorable memory: Making the study desk as dinning table, keeping all of our tiffins on it and having lots of options when we don't like our own tiffin :)

Mithibai College

Over here I had the class of Computer Science in which I learned the programming languages and how to code. It was my favorite class back then.

Memorable memory: I love beaches and my college was beach facing so I used to stand beside the window to feel at peace.

St. Francis Institute of Technology

During my undergrads, Database Management System was my favorite class as the professor used to give us few queries to solve at the start and end of the class.

Memorable memory: 15 days time period before the college technical and annual fest where we as an organizers used do the preparation of various events.
