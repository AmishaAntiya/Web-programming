Day I realized drawing is a stress buster for me

We all have experienced stress at some point of our life and how we tackle the situation decides our resiliency.
During my undergrads, there was this one subject which was incomprehensible for me. It went to a point where one day professor told me that it would greatly affect my overall grades. That was the last stroke to make me tensed. After coming back from college, I just started drawing and without even realising I made a mandala design. When I gave it a final touch I was flabbergasted because making a mandala was one of the task which I thought I'm not capable of till that moment (at the end is the glimpse of my mandala design). This lead me to think that nothing is impossible and I studied really hard for that subject. As a reasult I topped the final exam of that subject.
From then onwards whenever I feel low I start drawing the designs which I think I'm not capable of and the end result have always filled in positive energy to overcome all the hurdels.
